MindMate Community Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/09/2023

Welcome to MindMate Community

Welcome to MindMate Community, a mental health community app designed to provide a supportive platform for users to share their experiences, access helpful features, and connect with others in the pursuit of mental well-being. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the MindMate Community app and its associated services.

Privacy Policy Labeling

This document is the Privacy Policy for the MindMate Community app.

Entity Reference

This app is operated by MindMate Community, as named in the Google Play listing.

Privacy Point of Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at official.mindmate@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

Account Information

Registration Data: When you sign up for MindMate Community, we collect information such as your name, email address, and password.

User-Generated Content

Images and Posts: Users can post images and share their experiences. These posts may include personal information and content you choose to share.

App Usage Data

Usage Patterns: We collect information about how you use the app, including interactions with features like healing music, sleep tracker, mood tracker, daily journal, and positive affirmations.

Device Information

Device Identifiers: We collect device-specific information such as device type, operating system, and unique identifiers.

Location Information

Location Data: If you grant us permission, we may collect location information to provide location-based features and improve user experiences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Providing and enhancing the MindMate Community app and its features.
  • Offering personalized content and recommendations.
  • Responding to user inquiries and support requests.
  • Conducting research and analytics to improve our services.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

Sharing of Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may use third-party services to help us operate the app, including analytics, hosting, and customer support services.
  • Community Interaction: Your user-generated content may be visible to other users as part of the app's community features, such as comments and posts.
  • Legal Compliance: We may share information as required by law, such as responding to legal requests or protecting our rights.

Your Choices

You have control over your personal information. You can:

  • Edit your profile information.
  • Adjust app notification preferences.
  • Delete your account and associated data.

Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

MindMate Community is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from minors.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We have the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly. Please check this page periodically for updates. Your continued use of MindMate Community after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at official.mindmate@gmail.com.
